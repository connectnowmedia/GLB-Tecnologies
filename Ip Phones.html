Service Details

IP phones are devices that enable users to make phone calls over the internet protocol (IP) network, rather than traditional phone lines. They offer advanced features such as HD voice quality, video conferencing, and integration with other communication tools.

The Dhvani Series IP Phones from Global Technologies provide high-quality and flexible desktop telephony solutions for effective communication. With standard SIP support, dual gigabit ethernet ports, and a 4.3" colour display, the Dhvani series is ideal for any IP platform and offers features such as HD voice, 3-way conference, PoE support, and more.

IP3LGC – Gigabit Color IP Phone. The"Connect Series" IP Phones are a cost-effective and flexible enterprise-level desktop telephony solution that offers high-quality, feature-rich communication options. The Connect-IP3LGC is an executive class IP phone with a colour display and HD voice, designed to meet the needs of any IP platform.

IPDSS22 – IP Expansion Module. The Coral Connect – IPDSS22 is an IP-based Expansion Module for the Connect Series IP Phones that provides direct access to up to 60 programmable DSS keys in three pages. With a large graphical LCD display, programmable keys, and dual-color LEDs, it is ideal for operator/dispatcher terminals and disaster/emergency control rooms, supporting features such as BLF, Hotline, and Speed Dial.

Coral Connect-IPDSS22 is an IP-based expansion module designed for Connect series IP phones (IP3LPG, IP6LP, and IP6LPG) that features a large graphical LCD display, 20 programmable keys, and 20 dual-color LEDs. It can be programmed for various features, including BLF, BLF list, speed dial, hot desking, record, XML browser, account, call forward, and pickup. The IPDSS22 comes with a 4.3" 272x480 graphical LCD with backlight and 4-bit grayscale. It is powered by Dhvani and Connect Series IP phones and supports up to 60 programmable DSS keys in three pages. The keys also have a visual name display for easier identification. It can be programmed from IP phones and up to three IPDSS22 modules can be connected with Dhvani and Connect series IP phones (IP3LGC and IP6LP V3.0). The IPDSS22 is suitable for various applications such as IP expansion modules for direct access of users, operator/dispatcher terminals, disaster/emergency control rooms, BLF, hotline, and speed dial.

Sanchar is an innovative app-based unified communication client designed to provide efficient office communication management on your handheld mobile device. With its fully-featured application, Sanchar enables you to make voice and video calls over IP, send instant messages that can trigger tasks for your team, and collaborate more effectively within your organisation. With rich features such as receiving faxes, voice mails, and setting up video conference calls, Sanchar creates a virtual office that travels with you. The app offers directory services and enables you to receive, transfer, forward, and manage calls, making it an ideal office communication environment even when you are geographically distant. Sanchar offers a wide range of features, including incoming and outgoing calls, call hold, mute, call transfer, call forwarding, call back, DND, dynamic locking, FAX, auto answer, file sharing, follow-me, call history (missed/incoming/outgoing), instant messaging (IM), phonebook, corporate directory, presence, task alert, video calling, video conference (up to 16 parties), and voicemail. Sanchar is perfect for those who are always connected and want to work from home. It also offers audio conferencing, secure chatting, corporate directory, presence sharing, video calls, voicemail, and unified communication features to help streamline your office communication processes. Experience the power of Sanchar and manage your office communication efficiently, wherever you are!
