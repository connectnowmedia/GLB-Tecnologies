Service Details

Global Technologies offers IP-PBX (Internet Protocol - Private Branch Exchange) solutions that integrate voice, video and data communication over IP networks. IP-PBX replaces traditional telephone systems and offers greater flexibility, scalability and cost-effectiveness. Our IP-PBX solutions allow users to connect and collaborate from anywhere in the world through the internet, enabling remote working and reducing communication costs. Our systems also provide advanced call management features, such as call forwarding, voicemail, call recording and call reporting. With our IP-PBX solutions, businesses can enhance their communication capabilities while reducing costs and improving efficiency.

IRIS IVDX is an IP-based communications platform that follows open standard protocols, making it a versatile and flexible solution for any communication needs. With its multi-protocol media gateway architecture, it offers a hybrid solution that allows businesses to retain legacy hardware while transitioning to IP-based packet switching. The communication server supports and operates on open standards-based protocols such as H.248, H.323 and SIP, giving users the advantage of using standard SIP devices such as IP loggers, IP Dialers, billing packages, softphones, etc. The platform offers various features, including a disaster recovery server, inter-gateway alternate route (IGAR), voice mail with email notification, and pre-set conference calls. IRIS IVDX is ideal for large and medium corporate environments, multi-locational offices, mission-critical applications, railways, call centres, and more. Its legacy architecture allows businesses to migrate from TDM to pure IP at their pace while offering redundancy and flexibility.

Global Technologies offers IP-PBX systems that integrate voice and video conferencing, mobility, and next-gen telecom networks into a single solution. Our systems are innovative, reliable, and scalable, supporting all standard PBX features and IVR/auto-attendant services. With connectivity to all legacy and next-gen telecom networks, our IP-PBX helps businesses boost productivity and reduce costs. We offer solutions for mid-sized enterprises, as well as remote systems for multi-location offices. Connect your main office with all remote offices over the internet and access your extension from your mobile phone.
