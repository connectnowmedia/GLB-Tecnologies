Service Details


1. IRIS IVDX

Global Technologies offers an innovative IRIS IVDX Cloud solution with server-based cloud collaboration platforms that integrate voice, video, data, fax, and email services on the IP cloud. Our advanced technology offers converged communication capable of switching a variety of devices and seamlessly integrates with media gateways. This solution is an ideal choice for modern offices looking for reliable and efficient communication solutions. Contact us today to learn more.

2. IRIS IVDX Cloud Server Box

Global Technologies offers the Coral Cloud Server, a feature-rich collaboration platform for integrated voice, video, fax, and email services on a standard SIP-based soft-switch architecture. With options of 2 core or 4 core processors and a clock speed of 2.4 GHz, it can handle the traffic requirements of any mid-sized enterprise. Its powerful bus architecture supports PCIe bus to connect with 5G networks or to connect multiple PRI in future. The Coral Cloud Server is an ideal choice for modern enterprises looking for reliable and efficient communication solutions. Contact us today to learn more.

3. IP Operator Console

Global Technologies' IP Operator Console, Sahita, is the perfect solution for receptionists and operators who need to handle a high volume of calls efficiently. It provides call information and context-sensitive action buttons that enable the operator to respond quickly and appropriately to the caller. Coral IRIS IVDX cloud offers a range of options to empower operators to handle inbound and outbound traffic from specialised IP phones or a PC-based interface. The operator console allows for separate call queues for different types of incoming calls and provides a real-time status display of each user. It also offers a variety of telephony features such as consultation calls, 3-way conference calls, and operator-assisted services. The PC-based operator positions offer additional features such as sending real-time instant messages and offline sticky notes. Operators can perform call handling functions while minimising disruption to other tasks and can easily transfer calls with just a click of a mouse.

4. Network Management System

Global Technologies' Network Management System is designed to manage a wide range of IP devices. It offers discovery of all nodes, status tracking of each device, and real-time health monitoring. The NMS provides a single-page dashboard, alerts and notifications via email, SMS and WhatsApp. It also generates reports on various aspects such as CPU utilisation, RAM usage, disk usage and TCP errors. Roll-up facilities are available at various levels to facilitate better understanding of the network. The NMS can manage inventory and detect the addition or deletion of any IP device in the network.

5. Conference bridge

Our system provides high definition voice conferencing and video streaming capabilities for a rich unified communication experience. With dynamic conference controls, personal conference bridge, and auto-escalation options, users can easily manage conference calls and meetings. The system also includes voice recording and forwarding features, and can be integrated with booking and billing solutions for conference rooms.

6. Unified Communications

Global Technologies is at the forefront of providing innovative communication solutions to businesses around the world. Our collaboration platforms offer integrated voice, video, data, fax, and email services on the IP cloud, replacing outdated legacy systems. With our advanced technology, physical location is no longer a limitation, as users can be connected anywhere on the cloud. Our products are designed to meet the growing demands of converged switch markets and provide cutting-edge telecom solutions. We bring our rich experience in the telecom industry to offer businesses cost-effective solutions that make a lot of business sense. Join us in embracing the future of communication.

7. Connect Video Conference

Global Connect is a secure video collaboration platform that boosts employee efficiency and enables multimedia-based teamwork. It promotes a green work culture by reducing travel time and allows for remote work, leading to a better work-life balance and increased productivity. Join the trend towards a more sustainable and productive workplace with Global Connect.
